About

Hi, I'm Ilham—but you can call me Lam. I am a final-year Informatics student at Politeknik Negeri Manado, currently interning at PT Semesta Tegang Perkasa. I have a deep passion for blending clean code with striking visual aesthetics.

My work focuses on web development, particularly using React, alongside a strong interest in minimalist, typographic, and colorful design. Beyond coding, I explore creative visual storytelling through photography, videography, and video editing. Currently, I'm channeling these skills into my final thesis: building an integrated OTA platform with IoT for real-time underwater tourism monitoring.
